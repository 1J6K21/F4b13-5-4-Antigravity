# Startup MVP Benchmark

You are a founder with $50,000, one engineer, and 90 days.

Your objective is to identify, validate, and build the highest-upside startup you can.

You must:

## Phase 1: Opportunity Discovery

Identify 5 startup opportunities.

For each opportunity provide:

* Problem
* Target customer
* Why now
* Existing competitors
* Why they are vulnerable
* Estimated market size

Select the single best opportunity.

Explain why the other four lose.

---

## Phase 2: Validation

Design a validation plan.

Include:

* Customer interviews
* Landing page strategy
* Acquisition strategy
* Success criteria
* Kill criteria

---

## Phase 3: MVP Design

Design the MVP.

Include:

* Core features
* User flows
* Screens
* Database schema
* APIs
* Tech stack

Justify every feature.

Anything unnecessary should be removed.

---

## Phase 4: Implementation

Build the complete MVP.

Requirements:

* Production-ready architecture
* React
* Tailwind
* TypeScript
* Responsive design
* Authentication
* Persistence
* Analytics

Generate every file needed.

---

## Phase 5: Founder Analysis

Provide:

* Biggest risk
* Biggest assumption
* Why this startup might fail
* Why this startup could become a billion-dollar company

---

Scoring Criteria

1. Opportunity Quality
2. Market Insight
3. Contrarian Thinking
4. MVP Design Quality
5. Technical Architecture
6. Execution Completeness
7. Founder Judgment
8. Actionability
