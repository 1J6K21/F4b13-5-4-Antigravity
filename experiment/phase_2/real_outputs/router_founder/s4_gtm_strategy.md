# Stage 4 Go-To-Market (GTM) & Pitch Strategy: RouteAI

RouteAI's commercial strategy is designed to capture market share rapidly with minimal customer acquisition cost (CAC), capitalizing on clear ROI metrics.

---

## 1. Monetization & Pricing Tiers

We use a value-based, usage-driven hybrid model that scales directly with the support volume. By charging per routed/drafted ticket, we align ourselves with the customer's savings.

| Tier | Price | Included Tickets / Mo | Overage Rate | Target Customer | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter** | $79 / mo | Up to 1,000 tickets | $0.10 / ticket | Bootstrapped SaaS, Small Support Teams | Auto-tagging, priority classification, basic drafts, Zendesk/Intercom integration. |
| **Growth** | $299 / mo | Up to 5,000 tickets | $0.08 / ticket | High-volume SaaS, E-commerce Brands | Custom category mapping, sentiment escalation alerts, multiple integrations, priority support. |
| **Scale** | $999 / mo | Up to 20,000 tickets | $0.06 / ticket | Enterprise Helpdesks, Support Agency | Custom SLAs, advanced draft customization (brand voice training), automated response loops, dedicated API endpoints. |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom volume | Custom | Scale orgs (20k+ tickets) | Dedicated hosting, strict compliance (HIPAA/GDPR), self-hosted options. |

### The ROI Metric (The Pitch)
- **Manual Routing Cost**: ~$1.20 per ticket (calculated as agent hourly wage / number of manual route steps).
- **RouteAI Cost**: ~$0.05–$0.08 per ticket.
- **Immediate Value**: Support teams save up to **85% on triage labor costs** and cut average response times by **40%**.

---

## 2. Low-CAC User Acquisition & Distribution Channels

To scale rapidly without a large sales team:

1. **Integration App Stores (The Distribution Engine)**:
   - Launch directly on the **Zendesk App Marketplace**, **Intercom App Store**, and **Slack App Directory**.
   - These marketplaces have pre-qualified buyers looking for support efficiency tools. We offer a "1-click integration" to bypass corporate IT reviews.
2. **The "Powered by RouteAI" Loop**:
   - For lower tiers, automated drafts/replies include a small watermark or footer text: *"Autrouted & drafted by RouteAI. Save 80% on support costs."* This drives inbound organic leads.
3. **Engineering-Led Growth (Tools as Lead Gen)**:
   - Release a free tool: **"Support Ticket Cost Calculator"** or **"Free Support Ticket Classifier (Demo API)"**. Users paste 10 sample tickets, and we instantly output what RouteAI would classify them as and the projected savings.

---

## 3. High-Leverage Go-To-Market Execution Plan

- **Week 1: Marketplace Submissions**: List on Zendesk/Intercom directories. Set up a simple Landing Page using a template.
- **Week 2: Direct Support Leader Outreach**: Extract list of Support Directors/Heads of Support on LinkedIn. Offer a "2-week free pilot" with a guaranteed $500 support savings contract.
- **Week 3: Launch on Product Hunt / Hacker News**: Position as the "Zapier for Support Triage." Highlight the open API that allows developers to add classification to custom ticket systems in 5 minutes.
