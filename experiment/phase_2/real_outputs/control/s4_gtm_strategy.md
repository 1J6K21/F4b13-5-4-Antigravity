# Stage 4: Go-To-Market & Pitch Strategy

## 1. Value Pitch & Positioning
**ScribeAI** is positioned as the productivity multiplier for remote-first and fast-paced teams. By turning passive meeting audio into active project tasks, ScribeAI eliminates administrative friction and boosts team velocity.

---

## 2. Pricing Architecture (SaaS Model)

### **Free Tier (Individual)**
* **Cost:** $0 / user / month
* **Includes:**
  * 3 meeting uploads per month (max 45 mins per meeting)
  * Raw transcription & bulleted summary
  * Downloadable meeting summary PDFs

### **Pro Tier (Professional / Small Teams)**
* **Cost:** $15 / user / month (billed annually) or $19 / user / month (billed monthly)
* **Includes:**
  * Unlimited meeting uploads (up to 2 hours per meeting)
  * Advanced speaker detection & custom vocabulary dictionary
  * Action item automatic detection and assignee-tagging
  * Integrations: Slack, Zoom, Google Meet, Notion, and Google Drive

### **Enterprise Tier (Custom)**
* **Cost:** Custom pricing (based on volume / seats)
* **Includes:**
  * All Pro Tier features
  * Unlimited recording duration
  * Dedicated Virtual Assistant bot for real-time note-taking
  * Custom SAML/SSO authentication & strict data privacy constraints (HIPAA/GDPR compliance)
  * Dedicated Account Manager and 24/7 Priority Support

---

## 3. Go-To-Market (GTM) & User Acquisition Strategy

### A. Product-Led Growth (PLG)
* **Conferencing App Stores:** List ScribeAI on Zoom App Marketplace, Microsoft Teams Store, and Google Workspace Marketplace. This allows users to add the ScribeAI bot to their meetings with one click.
* **Viral Collaborative Invites:** When a user shares a ScribeAI meeting summary web link with external clients or internal team members, those viewers are invited to create a free account to edit/claim their action items.

### B. Content & SEO Marketing
* Build high-intent SEO content targeting queries related to team productivity, remote scrum ceremonies, and meeting efficiency (e.g., "How to write agile sprint minutes fast," "Templates for board meeting agendas").

### C. Developer & PM Community Outreach
* Sponsor product management newsletters, agile conferences, and podcasts (e.g., Lenny’s Podcast, Product Hunt launch).
* Offer free trials and exclusive discounts to tech accelerators (Y-Combinator, Techstars) and their portfolio companies.

### D. Direct Outbound Sales
* Target VP of Product, Engineering Directors, and Chief of Staff roles at mid-market companies (50-500 employees) to demo Enterprise workflows and integrations.
