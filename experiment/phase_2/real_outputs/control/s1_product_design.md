# Stage 1: Product Ideation & Design

## Product Name: **ScribeAI**

**ScribeAI** is an AI-powered SaaS product designed to automate meeting transcription, generate intelligent meeting minutes, and extract actionable follow-up items. It integrates with popular video conferencing tools to provide real-time or post-meeting summaries, ensuring team alignment and saving hours of administrative overhead.

---

## 1. Problem Statement
Teams spend valuable time in meetings and even more time writing summaries, tracking decisions, and manual action items. Key decisions get lost in long audio recordings or long chat histories, and non-attendees struggle to catch up quickly on what was discussed.

## 2. Value Proposition
* **Save Time:** Convert a 60-minute meeting into a readable 3-minute executive summary.
* **Accuracy:** Zero bias or human error in transcribing and summarizing.
* **Accountability:** Automatically detect, assign, and track action items to team members based on conversational context.

## 3. Target Audience
* Product Managers and Project Managers
* Engineering Leads and Scrum Masters
* Executive Assistants and Coordinators
* Remote and hybrid teams across all industries

## 4. Key Features
* **Real-time Integration:** Instantly connect to Zoom, Google Meet, and MS Teams via a virtual assistant bot or uploading audio/video recordings.
* **AI Summary Engine:** Generates multiple views of a meeting: full transcript, executive summary, key decisions, and a list of action items.
* **Auto Action-Item Assignment:** AI scans transcript context to assign action items to specific individuals (e.g., "John will deploy the fix by Friday" translates to a task for John Doe due on the upcoming Friday).
* **Workspace Collaboration:** Shareable meeting notes, searchable workspace, and integration with project management tools like Jira, Notion, and Slack.

## 5. Core User Flow
1. **Connect & Record:** User schedules a meeting or logs into ScribeAI dashboard to upload an audio recording.
2. **AI Processing:** ScribeAI transcribes the audio, runs summary models, and identifies speakers and action items.
3. **Review & Edit:** The meeting organizer reviews the generated summary, edits any incorrect details, and approves the action items.
4. **Distribute & Sync:** Approved meeting notes and tasks are automatically pushed to Slack, emailed to participants, and synced to project management software.
